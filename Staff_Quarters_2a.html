The man with the knife sharpener

You approach the man and ask him what he’s doing with the knife sharpener.
He replies, “It’s none of your business!” You explain to the
man that the captain has sent you to investigate a report of something suspicious
onboard the ship. It is with his authority that you are asking what he is doing
with the knife sharpener.The man replies, “Oh, I see. I’m real sorry about the
confrontation there. Wouldn’t want to upset the cap’n. I actually found
this on the portside deck. I can take you to the spot if you like?”
You reply, “Yes and please be quick about it!”
