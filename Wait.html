The mess hall is empty. But you can still see over the serving
counter into the kitchen.

You continue to listen to what’s being said. It has become clear
the head chef is the only person who knows about a ‘secret place’
on the ship. He tells the rest of the kitchen crew that he will return
shortly after he has checked on everything. You follow him after
he leaves the room. He heads toward the bow of the ship where he
approaches a door you’ve never seen before. The door has a fingerprint
scanner securing it shut. The head chef takes out a handkerchief from
his pocket and unfolds a human finger. He holds it to the scanner
and the door opens, granting him entry to the room.




The hinges on the door don’t look too sturdy.
You could probably kick it down.
Or you could revisit the captain and ask what’s in there.
What do you do?
