The sailor reading a book on his bunk

You approach the man and ask him if he has noticed anything suspicious
onboard the ship since it left port almost a week ago. He almost
jumps at you with relief! “Yes! Yes, I have!” he says. You ask him to
expand on what he has seen. His voice softens as to make sure he
isn’t overheard, “The man over there works in the kitchen crew. But he
has been standing over there sharpening knives all day. He didn’t
even go to his shift earlier and nobody came looking for him. That never
happens! Why would he do that? The other man carrying the knife sharpener
has not noticed your conversation. He picks up a bag of clinking metal and
heads out of the room, deeper into the ship. You thank the man who
has pointed him out to you and quietly follow the
clinking metal sounds down the corridor.
