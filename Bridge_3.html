You meet with the captain once more and explain what you’ve seen.
He immediately radios the coastguard to confirm the suspicions.
He tells them all of the details you’ve discovered and they deploy a
team to come to the ship. They also advise that the ship alters
it’s course to dock at the nearest port. Or at least get as close to
land as possible.




Once the conversation is over the captain comes back to you
and tells you to do whatever you can to save the ship!
